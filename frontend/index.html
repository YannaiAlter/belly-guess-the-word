How to Play 🎮
1

Connect your wallet

Use KasWare (recommended for IGRA Network) or MetaMask to join the game.

2

Read the hint

The game owner sets a secret word and provides a public hint. Use it to figure out the word.

3

Submit a guess

Type a single word and pay 10 iKAS. Your guess is verified on-chain — the plaintext is never stored on the blockchain.

4

Cooldown between guesses

Wait 10 seconds between each guess. You can submit up to 20 guesses per game.

5

Win the prize pool

The first player to guess correctly wins 95% of the accumulated prize pool. The remaining 5% is the operator fee.

⚠️ If no one wins within 1 day, the game resets and the pot is returned to the operator.
